# **App Name**: Roshan Community

## Core Features:

- Dashboard Overview: Display a summary of key information, including a Persian calendar, current time, and other relevant statistics.
- Personnel Management: Add, view, edit, and manage staff profiles including contact information and roles.
- Villa and Resident Management: Maintain records of villas, owners, and tenants (including separate tenant profiles).
- Interactive Schematic Map: Upload a schematic map with interactive elements. Clicking on a villa reveals resident and villa details, with options to edit.
- Board Member Management: Manage information about the board of directors (name, title, contact number).
- Guard Shift Management: Configure shift types (e.g., 12-hour, 8-hour).  The app acts as a tool that guard assignment and schedule creation based on shift types and guard availability to generate monthly shift schedules.
- Database: Store all app information in a Firestore database, ensuring data security and scalability.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep saffron (#FF9933), inspired by Iranian culture and the feeling of a sunset.
- Background color: Light tan (#F2E6D9), providing a warm and neutral canvas.
- Accent color: Teal (#3399FF) for interactive elements, as a calming complementary contrast to saffron.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font giving an elegant, intellectual feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a sans-serif combining modernity with warmth. Used with Alegreya.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent different management modules.
- Subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback on interactions.
